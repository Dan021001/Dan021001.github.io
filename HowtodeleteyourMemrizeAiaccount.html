If you wish to permanently delete your Memrize AI account and all associated data, you have two options:

Option 1: Delete directly inside the app

  1. Open the Memrize AI app.
  2. Go to your Profile.
  3. Tap Settings.
  4. Scroll all the way down and tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm by pressing Delete Account again when asked “Are you sure?”

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 7 days.
